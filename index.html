Introduction

This site is dedicated to the art piece, "Form Art" by Alexei Shulgin. Form Art was created in 1997 and has been archived on Rhizome Net Art Anthology.

Image Credit: Rhizome Net Art Anthology


In this section we'll see examples of how buttons were magically used in "Form Art" to create such an amazing art piece.






An example of Shulgin's style that I've recreated is shown above. Shulgin used a lot of buttons throughout the work to spell out words and create shapes. These buttons often linked to other pages which would contain patterns comprised of html or even a game Shulgin made.



Button Maze?!

There appears to be game or maze of buttons found in "Form Art". It can be found when click on the "Form" button next to the text box with "what:". Then click on one of the five text boxes at the bottom of the page.

More Styles and a Gomputer Game

On top of the button maze, there is an actual game in "Form Art" that uses a lot of html techniques. One of those is the check box.








On "Form Art", you can find the game by clicking on the buttons that spell out "Form" on the main page, then clicking on the button that says "Gomputer Game". Below is a video of the game.
